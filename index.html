Rook Finance

ROOK.Finance is a decentralized open source protocol that allows easy access to DeFi, STAKING, FARMING, GOVERNANCE VOTE, LOANS and LOANS, SWAP services from a simple dashboard.

STAKING

Other yield farming protocols have provided huge investment returns for whales, but leave little for the average investor. ROOK.Finance is the first performance agriculture platform that aims to curb inequality in performance agriculture.

LENDING

Our lending protocol aims to simplify the process of lending and borrowing crypto assets while maintaining decentralization and transparency by leveraging superior capacity (i.e. high performance and smart contract functionality).

GOVERNANCE

RKF members can participate in the governance of the network to maintain an open and fair ecosystem at ROOK.Finance. By holding RKF tokens, you are eligible to vote during the decision-making process. RKF users will collectively act as protocol governors.

ROOKSWAP

The ROOK platform will feature a decentralized exchange where users can easily swap token pairs at a reduced fee.

BORROW

Users are able to use their crypto assets as collateral to obtain a loan.

YIELD FARMING

Users on our platform will have the opportunity to put their crypto assets to work those assets. Our yield farming program will be launching in the fourth quarter of 2021.
